Vuece Hub

Vuece Hub is a free Windows app that hosts and streams your personal music files from your Windows PC to your Vuece Android Music Player, in real-time.

Vuece Hub supports global music streaming over Internet connection, it is not limited by your local network.

Vuece Hub also supports local streaming if your Hub and Vuece Player are in the same local network.

Vuece Hub supports streaming from more than one Android device.

Vuece Apps require no extra user account registration, if you have a Google account, you are already with us now :)

Vuece Hub setup is extremely simple. Simply link your Google account with Vuece Hub, drag & drop your music files into Vuece Hub, log in, that is it!

Vuece Apps are evolving, lots of amazing features are waiting for your to discover!

Vuece Music

Vuece Music Player is a powerful app that streams music from Vuece Music Hub to your Android device over Internet, in real-time.

With Vuece Music Player running on your Android device, you can remotely browse and play music on your or your friends' Vuece Hubs, no matter where you are.

To find out more about Vuece Music Player, meet us in Google Play Store.
