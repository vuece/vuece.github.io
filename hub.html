Vuece Hub

Link Account

Ready to get started with Vuece Hub? The first step is to link Vuece Hub with your current Google account

Step 1. Start Vuece Hub Application

Click 'Link Vuece Hub' to start the account linking procedure. If you don't have a Google account at all, click 'Sign up for a Google Account' or click here to start creating your Google account.

Step 2. Sign in with your Google Account

A dialog will pop up for your to sign in with your existing Google account, fill in your Google email address and password correctly, then click 'Sign in'

Step 3. Authorize

If sign in is successful, Vuece Hub application will ask for your permission to access some of your basic Google user information, click 'Allow' to proceed.

Step 4. Name your Hub

Congratulations! Your have successfully linked your Goolge account with Vuece Hub. You should see your Google user information is updated on Vuece Hub. Now it's time to give your new Vuece Hub a name so your friends can easily identify the owner of your Hub.

Share Folders

Before you can play any music on you Android device using Vuece Music app, you need to share your music files via Vuece Hub so you or your friends can remotely browse and play your music.

Step 1. Add music folders

Click 'Add' to choose and add your music folders in you PC, or simply drag and drop music folders to 'Public folder list' section.

Step 2. Scan music files

Click 'Scan', Vuece Hub will start scanning all the folders you have selected, this will take a while so please be patient, the time for scanning depends on how many songs you have selected.

Access Control

In 'Advanced' tab, you can decide whether or not to allow you friends to access and play your music file, and decide how may users can play your music concurrently.
